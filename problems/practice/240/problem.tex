\begin{prob}

    Suppose that a depth first search is being performed on an
    \textbf{undirected} graph, and when \mintinline{python}{dfs} is called on a node $u$,
    the statuses of all of its neighbors are printed. True or False:
    it is possible that one of the neighbors is marked as ``visited''.

    \tF{}

    \begin{soln}
        False
    \end{soln}

\end{prob}
