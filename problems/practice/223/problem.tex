\begin{prob}

    The adjacency matrix of a directed graph with nodes $u_1, \ldots, u_8$ is shown below:

    \[
        \begin{pmatrix}
            0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
            \textbf{1} & 0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
            0 & 0 & 0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
            0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
            0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 \\
            0 & 0 & 0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & \textbf{1} & 0 \\
            \textbf{1} & 0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & \textbf{1} \\
            \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & \textbf{1} & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
        \end{pmatrix}
    \]

    True or False: there is a path from node $u_1$ to node $u_8$.

    \Tf{}

    \begin{soln}
        True
    \end{soln}

\end{prob}
