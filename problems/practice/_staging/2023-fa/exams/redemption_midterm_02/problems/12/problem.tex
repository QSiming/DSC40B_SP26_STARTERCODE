\begin{prob}
    Suppose a connected, undirected graph $G$ has $n$ nodes, half of which are
    ``red'' and half of which are ``blue''. Each red node shares an edge with
    $\log_2 n$ blue nodes, and the only edges in the graph are between red and
    blue nodes.

    What is the time complexity of BFS when run on $G$? Your answer should be in asymptotic notation
    and in terms of $n$, in as simplest terms possible.

    \inlineresponsebox[.75in]{$\Theta(n \log n)$}

\end{prob}
