\begin{prob}

    Let $G$ be an undirected graph, and let $u$ and $v$ be two nodes in the graph, with $v$ reachable from
    $u$.

    Suppose that at the moment \python{dfs(u)} is called, $v$ is still
    undiscovered. True or False: the eventual start time of $v$ must be less
    than the finish time of $u$.

    \tF{}

\end{prob}
