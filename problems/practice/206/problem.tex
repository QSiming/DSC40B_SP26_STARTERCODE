\begin{prob}
    Suppose \mintinline{python}{bar_1}, \mintinline{python}{bar_2} and \mintinline{python}{bar_3} are three functions.
    Suppose \mintinline{python}{bar_1}'s time complexity is $\Theta(n)$, \mintinline{python}{bar_2}'s
    time complexity is $\Theta(n^2)$, and \mintinline{python}{bar_3}'s
    time complexity is $\Theta(n^3)$.

    Suppose \mintinline{python}{foo} is defined as below:

    \inputminted{python}{code.py}

    What is the asymptotic time complexity of \mintinline{python}{foo}?

    \inlineresponsebox{$\Theta(n^2)$}

    \begin{soln}
        Asymptotic time complexity concerns the time taken when $n$ is large.
        Therefore, it doesn't matter what happens when $n < 1000$.
        When $n \geq 1000$, the time taken is $\Theta(n^2)$, since that is the
        time taken by \mintinline{python}{bar_2}.
    \end{soln}
\end{prob}
